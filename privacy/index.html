Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

Coach Princeton Basketball ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at coachprincetonbasketball.com.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

  • Personal Data: Email address, name, and other contact information provided by you when purchasing products or subscribing to our services.
  • Financial Information: Payment information processed through our secure payment providers. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and time/date of visits through cookies and similar tracking technologies.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our content, which articles you read, and how long you spend on our site.

How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the Site to:

  • Process your purchases and send you related information
  • Send periodic emails regarding your order or other products and services
  • Improve customer service and support
  • Administer a contest, promotion, survey or other site feature
  • Analyze site traffic and user behavior
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities

Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations:

  • By Law or to Protect Rights: If required by law or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the rights of others.
  • Service Providers: To vendors, consultants, and service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business.
  • Third-Party Advertisers: We may share de-identified usage information with advertising partners.

Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to help personalize your experience on our site. Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. If you prefer, you can usually choose to set your browser to remove cookies and to reject cookies. Please note that if you choose to remove or reject cookies, this could affect the availability and functionality of our site.

Third-Party Websites

The Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites before providing your personal information.

Email Marketing

If you opt in to receive emails from us, we will use your email address to send you marketing communications about our products and services. You can unsubscribe from these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or by contacting us directly.

Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

California Privacy Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information. If you are a California resident, you have the right to request what personal information we collect, how we use it, and with whom we share it. You also have the right to request deletion of your personal information.
