Coach Lee DeForest

Coach Lee DeForest has spent 25 years in basketball, serving as a coach, mentor, and innovator at the highest levels of the sport. Currently, he serves as Athletic Director and Director of Operations at Florida Coastal Prep, where he oversees a comprehensive basketball program dedicated to player development and college placement.

Throughout his career, Coach DeForest has coached at NCAA Division I, Division II, NAIA, and JUCO levels. He's developed numerous players who have gone on to compete at the college level, including successful placements at Missouri, DePaul, Houston Baptist, and SIU Edwardsville.

Beyond coaching on the court, Coach DeForest is a published author on basketball offense strategy and has created instructional DVD materials that have influenced coaches worldwide. His expertise in the Princeton Offense—a read-based, system-oriented approach to basketball—has earned him recognition as one of the sport's most innovative offensive minds.

Coaching Philosophy

Coach DeForest's approach to basketball is built on developing intelligent, confident players who understand the "why" behind every possession. The Princeton Offense is the foundation of this philosophy.

Basketball IQ First

Great basketball is about reading the defense and making the right decision. Coach DeForest teaches players to think, not just execute plays. A thinking player is unstoppable.

Systematic Approach

The Princeton Offense provides a complete system with six sets, fourteen counters, and infinite scoring opportunities. Coaches who use this system find themselves spending less time designing plays and more time developing players.

Works at Every Level

Coach DeForest has proven this system at middle school, high school, college, and professional levels. It scales from youth to elite competition. It's not about talent—it's about system and execution.

College Placement Focus

His programs consistently place players at college programs because the Princeton Offense develops the basketball skills and IQ that college coaches look for. Smart, skilled, confident players get recruited.

Complete Player Development

Running the Princeton Offense develops better shooters, better decision-makers, better ball handlers. It creates complete basketball players, not just specialists who can execute one role.

Proven Results

200+ coaches worldwide have adopted this system. Players placed at D1, D2, NAIA, and JUCO programs. Coaches seeing immediate offensive improvement. Real results, real coaches, real teams.
